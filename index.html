Leipzig excursion 2024

Hello world! This webpage presents a collection of sketches and photos I made during my uni trip to Leipzig, Germany, 2024. I hope you will enjoy them!

To begin with, I will show you a small gallery of photos I did during the whole week to present my impression from Leipzig.

I would like to continue with the sketches I made during our walk around Leipzig on the first day. When creating these sketches, I wanted to make them complement each other, revealing the landscape. That's why I did every two of them from the same point.

I found the main exposition of the Museum of fine arts Leipzig especially interesting. I'm into older drawing movements, so analyse of presented works helped me as well to understand, how artists manage to create the atmosphere, playing with light and shadow.

Our next stop was the Galerie für Zeitgenössische Kunst Leipzig. This gallery gave me a feeling of nostalgia for some reason, so in my sketches I looked at the exhibition from the point of view of a person, who moved from their country. Therefore, in the presented works I looked for my personal associations.

As a part of the photography festival in Leipzig, we visited several exhibitions, including an exhibition of works at the Hochschule für Grafik und Buchkunst. The presented works were devoted to very different topics. I was especially interested in the audio recording about person and society, notes for which you will find below.

Several galleries and a large number of artists have settled on the site of the former Leipzig cotton spinning mill. The atmosphere of this place just called for me to pick up a pencil and start creating.
